Our observations

When it does not exclude them from the labour market outright, deafness still represents a handicap for hard of hearing and deaf people who want to access employment and pursue a career.

According to them, deafness is associated with devaluing prejudices and is a reason for non-recruitment or non-access to certain positions. It would subject them to different treatment in the workplace. But their main problem remains the lack of communication and isolation.
